Who We Are

The Bangladesh Welfare Community Bahrain (BWCBH) is a dedicated community organization committed to serving and supporting Bangladeshi expatriates living and working in the Kingdom of Bahrain.

Since our establishment in January 2020, we have been consistently working towards the welfare, rights, and well-being of our community members, providing assistance, guidance, and support whenever needed.

"The Bangladesh Welfare Community Bahrain has consistently been working for the welfare of expatriates. Heartfelt thanks to Mr. Taj Uddin Sir for his dedicated contributions."
