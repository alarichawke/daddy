About Us

Who We Are

Bangla Song Appreciation (BSA) is more than just a community — it's a movement dedicated to honoring the timeless beauty of Bengali music and the artists who create it.

Our Story

Founded in 2014, Bangla Song Appreciation started as a small group of passionate music lovers who wanted to create a space where Bengali songs could be celebrated, discussed, and shared with the world.

Over the years, we have grown into a recognized community with an official trademark registration under the Department of Patents, Designs and Trademarks (DPDT), Bangladesh. Our trademark number 193586 stands as a testament to our legitimacy and commitment.

Under the leadership of Aheen Afnan, President of BSA, we continue to expand our reach and impact in the Bengali music scene, connecting artists, listeners, and enthusiasts from all walks of life.

Our Work

What We Do

BSA focuses on various aspects of Bengali music — from appreciation and promotion to preservation and community building. Here's how we contribute to the music scene.

Music Appreciation

We celebrate Bengali songs across all genres — from classical Rabindra Sangeet and Nazrul Geeti to modern pop, rock, folk, and band music. Every song has a story, and we help tell it.

Community Building

We bring together music lovers, artists, composers, and lyricists under one roof. Our community provides a platform for meaningful discussions, collaborations, and connections.

Artist Promotion

We support both emerging and established Bengali artists by promoting their work, sharing their music, and helping them reach a wider audience through our platform.

Cultural Preservation

Bengali music is a treasure of our culture. We work to preserve rare and classic songs, ensuring that future generations can experience the beauty of our musical heritage.

Song Reviews & Analysis

We provide thoughtful reviews and in-depth analysis of Bengali songs — exploring lyrics, composition, melody, and the emotions they evoke in listeners.

Content Sharing

Through our platforms, we share curated playlists, song recommendations, artist spotlights, and music-related content to keep our community engaged and informed.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a vibrant and inclusive community where Bengali music is celebrated, appreciated, and shared. We aim to:

  • Promote Bengali songs and artists globally
  • Connect music lovers through meaningful engagement
  • Preserve the rich heritage of Bengali music
  • Support emerging artists and musicians

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for Bengali music appreciation worldwide. We envision a future where:

  • Bengali music reaches every corner of the world
  • Artists receive the recognition they deserve
  • Music becomes a bridge connecting generations
  • Our cultural heritage thrives for centuries
